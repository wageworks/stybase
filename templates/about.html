About stybase

stybase is a unified login and authentication platform designed to streamline access across multiple websites and apps. With stybase, you can create a single account and securely log in to all integrated platforms, including our own services like Sociocon.

Developers can register their applications with stybase to offer seamless OAuth login for users, while users can manage which apps have access to their account from their dashboard.

Our Mission

To simplify digital identity management and empower developers with a secure, easy-to-use OAuth platform.
